This library is not really big but the whole room is filled with bookselves with books that kids enjoy. Everyday a kid would come to the library and take a book to read after school and the next day you would see them bring it back.

This is the schools auditorium it is a big area. We give the kids their awards up here on the stage. It is used for the plays that the kids do. A lot of parents come to see the award ceremony when some big event has happened.
